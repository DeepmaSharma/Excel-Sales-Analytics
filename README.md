# Excel Sales & Finance Analysis

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales and financial data using Excel to generate meaningful insights that support business decision-making. The reports are designed to track performance, compare targets with actuals, and present financial outcomes in a clear and actionable format.

---

## Sales Analysis

### Objectives

* Build a detailed **Customer Performance Report** to evaluate customer-wise sales trends and contributions.
* Compare **market performance against sales targets** to identify gaps and growth opportunities.

### Why Sales Analytics Matters

* Helps organizations continuously track and review sales performance.
* Enables identification of trends, patterns, and key performance indicators (KPIs).

### Value of Sales Reports

* Support data-driven discount strategies.
* Strengthen negotiation with customers using performance insights.
* Highlight high-potential markets for future business expansion.

---

## Finance Analysis

### Objectives

* Prepare **Profit & Loss (P&L) statements** segmented by fiscal year and by month.
* Analyze **P&L performance across different markets**.

### Purpose of Financial Analytics

* Assess overall financial health and profitability.
* Assist leadership in strategic and operational decision-making.
* Improve transparency and communication with stakeholders.

### Importance of Financial Reports

* Enable comparison with previous periods and industry benchmarks.
* Provide a strong base for budgeting, forecasting, and planning.
* Ensure financial strategies are aligned with long-term business goals.

## Technical Skills Demonstrated

* ETL process implementation (Extract, Transform, Load).
* Creation of dynamic date tables using Power Query.
* Derivation of fiscal months and quarters.
* Building and managing data model relationships with Power Pivot.
* Integrating additional datasets into an existing data model.
* Using DAX to create calculated columns and enhance analysis.
